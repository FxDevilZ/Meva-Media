Bali Beach Restaurant - Complete Digital Transformation

This beachfront restaurant in Seminyak was struggling with low online visibility and inconsistent social media presence. They needed a complete digital transformation to compete in Bali's competitive restaurant scene.

Our Approach:

  • Developed a comprehensive content strategy focusing on visual storytelling
  • Created consistent brand identity across all social media platforms
  • Implemented local SEO strategies to improve search visibility
  • Designed and developed a modern, mobile-responsive website
  • Established ongoing social media management and community engagement

Results:

250% Social Media Engagement
180% Follower Growth
40% Online Reservations
300% Website Traffic
