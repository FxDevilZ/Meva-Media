Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Social media campaigns typically start within 1-2 weeks, while website development projects take 4-8 weeks on average. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your pricing?

Our pricing includes strategy development, content creation, design work, implementation, and ongoing support. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees, and all packages include performance reporting and optimization.

Do you work with businesses outside of Bali?

Yes! While we're based in Bali, we work with businesses across Indonesia and internationally. Our digital services are not limited by geography, and we can effectively serve clients anywhere in the world.

What makes MEVA Media different from other agencies?

We combine local market knowledge with international best practices, provide personalized service rather than cookie-cutter solutions, and focus on measurable results. Our team is dedicated to building long-term partnerships with our clients.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Absolutely! We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients. We offer ongoing support, maintenance, and optimization services to ensure your digital presence continues to perform and grow.

How do you measure success for your clients?

We track key performance indicators (KPIs) specific to your business goals, including engagement rates, website traffic, conversion rates, and ROI. We provide regular reports and insights to show the impact of our work.
