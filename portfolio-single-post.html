Bali Restaurant Campaign

Complete social media transformation for a local restaurant chain in Bali, Indonesia

Project Date: March 2024
Duration: 3 Months
Industry: Food & Beverage

Project Overview

A local restaurant chain in Bali was struggling with their social media presence and online engagement. They approached MEVA Media to completely transform their digital marketing strategy and increase their customer base through social media platforms.

The Challenge

  • Low social media engagement (less than 2% engagement rate)
  • Inconsistent posting schedule
  • Poor visual content quality
  • No clear brand voice or strategy
  • Limited online presence in competitive Bali market

Our Solution

  • Comprehensive social media strategy development
  • Professional content creation and visual design
  • Consistent posting schedule with automated tools
  • Engagement-focused content strategy
  • Local influencer partnerships and collaborations
